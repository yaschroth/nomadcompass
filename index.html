Each city is rated on 13 categories using a combination of official data sources, global indices, and expert assessments. The Nomad Score is the average of all category scores (1-10 scale).

1
Climate
Temperature, humidity, rain days, and seasonal extremes. Optimized for remote work comfort (15-28°C ideal).
2
Cost
Monthly living expenses including rent, food, transport, and entertainment. Based on Numbeo Cost of Living Index.
3
WiFi
Average internet speed and reliability. Based on Speedtest Global Index and coworking infrastructure.
4
Nightlife
Bars, clubs, live music, and social scene vibrancy. Based on venue density and reputation.
5
Nature
Access to parks, beaches, mountains, and outdoor activities. Based on proximity and diversity of natural attractions.
6
Safety
Personal safety and crime rates. Based on Numbeo Crime Index (inverted) and government travel advisories.
7
Food
Culinary scene quality and diversity. Based on Michelin recognition, local cuisine reputation, and street food culture.
8
Community
Digital nomad presence and networking opportunities. Based on coworking spaces, meetups, and expat communities.
9
English
English proficiency among locals. Based on EF English Proficiency Index and tourism infrastructure.
10
Visa
Ease of obtaining visa or staying long-term. Considers digital nomad visas, visa-free stays, and extension options.
11
Culture
Museums, galleries, historical sites, and cultural events. Based on UNESCO sites and cultural vibrancy.
12
Cleanliness
Street cleanliness, waste management, and overall city hygiene standards.
13
Air Quality
Air pollution levels and AQI. Based on IQAir World Air Quality Report and WHO standards.
Score Scale
8-10: Excellent
6-7: Good
4-5: Average
1-3: Below Average
