Best Neighborhoods in Ericeira

Where to base yourself for the perfect nomad experience

Centro/Old Town

$$
Traditional & Charming

Ericeira's whitewashed fishing heart

Ericeira's center is a charming fishing village - whitewashed houses, cobblestone streets, and Atlantic views. It's authentic Portuguese coast.

Best for: Those seeking authentic Portugal with surf access
✓ Pros
  • Charming village
  • Good restaurants
  • Atlantic views
  • Walkable
✗ Cons
  • Small center
  • Limited nightlife
  • Can be windy

Ribeira d'Ilhas Area

$$
Surf & Active

Ericeira's world-class surf zone

Ribeira d'Ilhas is Ericeira's surf mecca - World Surf Reserve beach, surf schools, and wave-focused community. It's why surfers come to Portugal.

Best for: Surfers, surf learners, and those seeking wave culture
✓ Pros
  • World-class waves
  • Surf schools
  • Surf community
  • Beautiful beach
✗ Cons
  • Crowded lineups
  • Surf-focused only
  • Needs transport

Foz do Lizandro

$
Family & Beginner Surf

Ericeira's family beach south

Foz do Lizandro is Ericeira's southern beach - river mouth, beginner waves, and family atmosphere. Mellower than the main surf breaks.

Best for: Families, beginner surfers, and those seeking mellow beaches
✓ Pros
  • Beginner waves
  • Family-friendly
  • River beach
  • Surf schools
✗ Cons
  • Far from center
  • Limited dining
  • Seasonal crowd

São Sebastião

$
Residential & Local

Ericeira's local residential hills

São Sebastião is where Ericeira's long-term visitors live - quieter streets, local markets, and residential atmosphere above the town.

Best for: Long-term stayers, digital nomads seeking quiet
✓ Pros
  • Quieter
  • Local markets
  • Affordable rent
  • Residential peace
✗ Cons
  • Uphill walk
  • Limited dining
  • Need transport for surf

Praia dos Pescadores

$$
Town Beach & Traditional

Ericeira's central fisherman beach

Praia dos Pescadores is Ericeira's town beach - small cove below the village with fishing boats and beach bars. Central and atmospheric.

Best for: Those wanting beach access from village center
✓ Pros
  • Central location
  • Fishing boats
  • Beach bars
  • Village access
✗ Cons
  • Small beach
  • Crowded summer
  • Rocky areas
