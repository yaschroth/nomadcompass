Best Neighborhoods in Alicante

Where to base yourself for the perfect nomad experience

Casco Antiguo/Barrio

$
Historic & Colorful

Alicante's colorful old town

Alicante's old town climbs beneath the Santa Bárbara castle - narrow streets, tapas bars, and Mediterranean character. It's authentic coastal Spain.

Best for: Tapas lovers, history seekers, and those wanting old Alicante
✓ Pros
  • Castle access
  • Tapas bars
  • Colorful streets
  • Authentic
✗ Cons
  • Hilly
  • Can be rough
  • Hot summers

Explanada/Port Area

$$
Promenade & Social

Alicante's famous palm promenade

The Explanada de España is Alicante's heart - marble wave promenade, port terraces, and evening paseo. It's the city's social soul.

Best for: Evening strollers, port lovers, and those seeking social Alicante
✓ Pros
  • Famous promenade
  • Port dining
  • Social scene
  • Beautiful
✗ Cons
  • Touristy
  • Expensive drinks
  • Crowded

Playa de San Juan

$$
Beach & Resort

Alicante's main beach strip

San Juan Beach is Alicante's best - 7km of golden sand, beach bars, and summer atmosphere. It's where Alicante's beach life happens.

Best for: Beach lovers, families, and those wanting sand and sun
✓ Pros
  • Best beach
  • Long stretch
  • Beach bars
  • Clean sand
✗ Cons
  • Crowded summer
  • Far from center
  • Resort atmosphere

Centro/Mercado Central

$
Commercial & Local

Alicante's commercial heart

Alicante's center is the commercial hub - Mercado Central, shopping streets, and everyday Spanish life. It's practical Alicante.

Best for: Shoppers, market lovers, and those wanting local Alicante
✓ Pros
  • Mercado Central
  • Shopping
  • Local life
  • Practical
✗ Cons
  • Less scenic
  • Hot summer
  • Commercial

Santa Bárbara Castle

$
Historic & Viewpoint

Alicante's commanding hilltop fortress

Santa Bárbara dominates Alicante - medieval fortress with panoramic views over city and sea. It's essential for understanding the city.

Best for: History lovers, view seekers, and those wanting panoramas
✓ Pros
  • Best views
  • Free entry
  • Historic fortress
  • Lift access
✗ Cons
  • Hot climb
  • Crowded peak times
  • Limited shade
