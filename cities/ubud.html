Best Neighborhoods in Ubud

Where to base yourself for the perfect nomad experience

Central Ubud

$$
Cultural & Central

Ubud's cultural temple heart

Central Ubud is the cultural core - Royal Palace, art market, and main streets. It's touristy but essential for Ubud's cultural experiences.

Best for: Culture lovers, first-time visitors, and those seeking central convenience
✓ Pros
  • Royal Palace
  • Art market
  • Restaurants
  • Dance performances
✗ Cons
  • Very touristy
  • Traffic
  • Crowded streets

Penestanan

$$
Artistic & Peaceful

Ubud's artistic hillside retreat

Penestanan is Ubud's original artist colony - peaceful rice terraces, yoga studios, and creative community. It's where Ubud's bohemian spirit lives on.

Best for: Artists, yoga practitioners, and those seeking peaceful Ubud
✓ Pros
  • Rice terrace views
  • Yoga studios
  • Artistic community
  • Peaceful
✗ Cons
  • Steep access
  • Far from center
  • Limited dining

Nyuh Kuning

$
Village & Authentic

Ubud's authentic village escape

Nyuh Kuning is a real Balinese village - monkey forest edge, local craftspeople, and authentic atmosphere. It's Ubud without the tourist crowds.

Best for: Long-term stayers, culture seekers, and those seeking real Bali
✓ Pros
  • Authentic village
  • Near monkey forest
  • Local crafts
  • Peaceful
✗ Cons
  • Limited restaurants
  • Quiet evenings
  • Need scooter

Campuhan/Sanggingan

$$$
Wellness & Scenic

Ubud's ridge walk wellness zone

Campuhan is Ubud's wellness hub - the famous ridge walk, luxury spas, and stunning river valley views. It's where Ubud's spiritual tourism began.

Best for: Wellness seekers, walkers, and those seeking scenic Ubud
✓ Pros
  • Famous ridge walk
  • Luxury spas
  • Valley views
  • Peaceful
✗ Cons
  • Expensive stays
  • Limited budget options
  • Spread out

Tegallalang

$$
Scenic & Tourist

Ubud's iconic rice terrace region

Tegallalang is home to Ubud's most photographed rice terraces - stunning views, swing attractions, and craft villages. Beautiful but increasingly commercialized.

Best for: Photographers, day trippers, and those seeking iconic views
✓ Pros
  • Iconic terraces
  • Amazing photos
  • Craft villages
  • Cafes with views
✗ Cons
  • Very touristy
  • Entrance fees everywhere
  • Far from Ubud center
