Best Neighborhoods in Zurich

Where to base yourself for the perfect nomad experience

Kreis 4 (Aussersihl)

$$
Multicultural & Nightlife

Zurich's multicultural nightlife district

Kreis 4 is Zurich's most diverse neighborhood - Langstrasse nightlife, multicultural restaurants, and edgy character. It's Zurich's least Swiss feeling area, which for many is exactly the appeal.

Best for: Nightlife lovers, diversity seekers, and nomads wanting less sterile Zurich
✓ Pros
  • Best nightlife
  • Multicultural food
  • More affordable
  • Character
✗ Cons
  • Red-light heritage
  • Noisy
  • Some rough areas

Kreis 5 (Industriequartier)

$$$
Creative & Industrial

Zurich's creative hub in former factories

Kreis 5 is Zurich's creative transformation - industrial spaces turned galleries, restaurants, and tech hubs. It's where Zurich's innovation happens, with growing nomad and startup scene.

Best for: Creatives, tech workers, and nomads seeking innovative Zurich
✓ Pros
  • Creative scene
  • Industrial cool
  • Good restaurants
  • Growing tech hub
✗ Cons
  • Expensive
  • Industrial aesthetic
  • Developing area

Niederdorf (Altstadt)

$$$
Historic & Tourist

Zurich's medieval old town with character

Niederdorf is Zurich's old town east of the Limmat - medieval streets, traditional restaurants, and historic atmosphere. It's touristy but characterful, with Zurich's most distinctive setting.

Best for: History lovers, tourists, and nomads wanting old Zurich charm
✓ Pros
  • Historic streets
  • Central location
  • Good restaurants
  • Character
✗ Cons
  • Touristy
  • Expensive
  • Limited modern amenities

Seefeld

$$$
Elegant & Lakeside

Zurich's lakeside elegance

Seefeld is Zurich's elegant lakeside neighborhood - lake access, upscale dining, and refined atmosphere. It's where wealthy Zurichers live, with opera house and beautiful promenades.

Best for: Lake lovers, the affluent, and nomads seeking refined Zurich living
✓ Pros
  • Lake access
  • Elegant dining
  • Beautiful promenades
  • Opera house
✗ Cons
  • Very expensive
  • Quiet evenings
  • Exclusive feel

Wiedikon

$$
Residential & Practical

Zurich's residential value neighborhood

Wiedikon offers Zurich's best residential value - good infrastructure, local character, and proximity to center without premium prices. It's where practical Zurichers live.

Best for: Budget nomads, long-term stayers, and those seeking practical Zurich living
✓ Pros
  • Better value
  • Good infrastructure
  • Local feel
  • Near center
✗ Cons
  • Less exciting
  • Residential
  • Basic character
