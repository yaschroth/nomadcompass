Best Neighborhoods in Phu Quoc

Where to base yourself for the perfect nomad experience

Duong Dong

$
Local & Central

Phu Quoc's main town hub

Duong Dong is Phu Quoc's heart - night market, local restaurants, and main services. It's the island's most developed area with authentic Vietnamese character.

Best for: Food lovers, night market seekers, and those wanting local Phu Quoc
✓ Pros
  • Night market
  • Local food
  • Services
  • Central
✗ Cons
  • No beach
  • Traffic
  • Development chaos

Long Beach

$$
Beach & Resort

Phu Quoc's main resort strip

Long Beach stretches south of Duong Dong - resorts, beach bars, and the island's main tourist infrastructure. Sunsets here are Phu Quoc's best.

Best for: Beach lovers, sunset seekers, and those wanting resort access
✓ Pros
  • Beautiful sunsets
  • Beach resorts
  • Restaurants
  • Beach bars
✗ Cons
  • Touristy
  • Expensive
  • Crowded beaches

Ong Lang Beach

$$
Boutique & Quiet

Phu Quoc's quieter boutique stretch

Ong Lang offers Phu Quoc's best balance - boutique resorts, quieter beaches, and good restaurants without Long Beach crowds.

Best for: Couples, boutique hotel lovers, and those seeking quiet beaches
✓ Pros
  • Quieter
  • Boutique resorts
  • Good restaurants
  • Nice beach
✗ Cons
  • Far from town
  • Limited services
  • Transport needed

Sao Beach Area

$$
Paradise & Popular

Phu Quoc's postcard-perfect south

Sao Beach has Phu Quoc's whitest sand and clearest water - postcard perfect but increasingly developed. Worth the trip for the beach quality.

Best for: Beach quality seekers, photographers, and day trippers
✓ Pros
  • White sand
  • Clear water
  • Beautiful
  • Instagram perfect
✗ Cons
  • Crowded
  • Development encroaching
  • Day trip crowds

An Thoi/South Island

$$
Adventure & Island

Phu Quoc's cable car and islands

An Thoi is Phu Quoc's southern tip - world's longest cable car to Hon Thom island, port for island hopping, and fishing village character.

Best for: Cable car riders, island hoppers, and those seeking adventure
✓ Pros
  • Cable car
  • Island hopping
  • Fishing village
  • Less touristy
✗ Cons
  • Far south
  • Cable car expensive
  • Limited accommodation
