Best Neighborhoods in Antalya

Where to base yourself for the perfect nomad experience

Kaleiçi

$$$
Historic & Charming

Antalya's enchanting old town

Kaleiçi is a perfectly preserved Ottoman old town - narrow cobblestone streets, historic houses converted to boutique hotels, and the ancient harbor create a magical atmosphere for digital nomads seeking inspiration.

Best for: History lovers, photographers, and those wanting old-world Turkish atmosphere
✓ Pros
  • Stunning architecture
  • Harbor views
  • Boutique hotels
  • Central location
✗ Cons
  • Touristy
  • Limited modern amenities
  • Cobblestones challenging

Lara Beach

$$$$
Resort & Beach

Antalya's premium beach resort zone

Lara Beach is where Antalya goes luxury - mega resorts, golden sand beaches, and all-inclusive everything. It's removed from the city but perfect for those wanting resort-style living.

Best for: Beach lovers, resort seekers, and those wanting all-inclusive convenience
✓ Pros
  • Beautiful beach
  • Luxury resorts
  • Good facilities
  • Family-friendly
✗ Cons
  • Far from city
  • Expensive
  • Touristy bubble

Konyaaltı

$$
Modern & Coastal

Antalya's modern beach district

West of the center, Konyaaltı stretches along a 7km pebble beach - modern apartments, waterfront cafes, and the dramatic Taurus Mountains as a backdrop. It's where expats and modern Turks live.

Best for: Long-term nomads, expats, and those wanting modern comforts with beach access
✓ Pros
  • Long beach
  • Modern amenities
  • Mountain views
  • Good value
✗ Cons
  • Pebble beach
  • Spread out
  • Less character

Muratpaşa Center

$
Urban & Local

Antalya's urban heart

The modern city center offers Turkish urban life - shopping streets, local restaurants, and real city energy. It's less photogenic but more authentic and affordable than the tourist zones.

Best for: Budget nomads, Turkish culture seekers, and those wanting local life
✓ Pros
  • Affordable
  • Authentic
  • Good transport
  • Shopping
✗ Cons
  • Less scenic
  • No beach access
  • Busy streets

Kepez

$
Local & Affordable

Affordable residential Antalya

Kepez is Antalya's affordable sprawl - modern apartments, local neighborhoods, and authentic Turkish daily life. It's where you live like a local and stretch your budget.

Best for: Long-term budget nomads, Turkish language learners, and value seekers
✓ Pros
  • Very affordable
  • Authentic
  • Local markets
  • Good apartments
✗ Cons
  • Far from beaches
  • Less English spoken
  • Basic
