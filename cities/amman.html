Best Neighborhoods in Amman

Where to base yourself for the perfect nomad experience

Downtown (Al-Balad)

$
Historic & Authentic

Ancient souks and Roman ruins

Downtown Amman is the city's historic heart - Roman amphitheater, traditional souks, gold markets, and falafel shops. Authentic Amman at its most vibrant.

Best for: Culture seekers, history lovers, and authentic experience hunters
✓ Pros
  • Roman ruins
  • Authentic souks
  • Great food
  • Affordable
✗ Cons
  • Chaotic traffic
  • Can be overwhelming
  • Few hotels

Jabal Amman

$$
Bohemian & Trendy

Bohemian hillside with Rainbow Street

Jabal Amman combines old and new - Rainbow Street's cafés and galleries, traditional houses, panoramic views. Where young Amman creates and socializes.

Best for: Young travelers, café lovers, and art enthusiasts
✓ Pros
  • Great cafés
  • Art galleries
  • City views
  • Cool atmosphere
✗ Cons
  • Hilly terrain
  • Parking difficult
  • Can be noisy

Abdoun

$$$
Upscale & International

Upscale expat enclave with modern dining

Abdoun is where Amman's wealthy and expats live - villas, international restaurants, embassy row. Where modern dining and cocktails are possible.

Best for: Expats, luxury seekers, and those wanting international options
✓ Pros
  • Fine dining
  • Modern cafés
  • Safe area
  • International feel
✗ Cons
  • Expensive
  • Removed from culture
  • Car needed

Sweifieh

$$
Modern & Commercial

Shopping malls and middle-class modernity

Sweifieh represents modern Amman - shopping malls, chain restaurants, middle-class living. Practical and accessible for everyday needs.

Best for: Shoppers, practical travelers, and those wanting convenience
✓ Pros
  • Good shopping
  • Modern amenities
  • Accessible
  • Good transport
✗ Cons
  • Generic feel
  • Traffic congestion
  • No historic charm

Citadel Area (Jabal al-Qal'a)

$
Ancient & Panoramic

Ancient hilltop overlooking the city

The Citadel hill holds Amman's ancient secrets - ruins spanning Bronze Age to Umayyad, Temple of Hercules, panoramic views. The city's spiritual heart.

Best for: History enthusiasts, photographers, and view seekers
✓ Pros
  • Incredible history
  • Amazing views
  • Archaeological sites
  • Cultural depth
✗ Cons
  • Exposed heat
  • Limited shade
  • Tourism focused
